Hisabify – Smart Expense Tracker

Full-Stack | Finance | MERN | Automation

Hisabify is a personal finance tracking system that helps users manage expenses, track categories, generate insights, and automate budgeting.

Role: Full-Stack Developer

Duration: 10 Weeks

Tools: React, Node.js, MongoDB, JWT, Chart.js

1. The Problem

People struggle to track everyday expenses and often lose clarity about where money is going.

Goal: Make personal finance simple, automated, and visual.

2. Solution

  • Smart expense categorization
  • Daily/Weekly/Monthly analytics
  • JWT based secure login/signup
  • Export to CSV and PDF
  • Auto reminders + savings suggestions

3. Key Results

  • 80% users reported better money clarity
  • Reduced expense tracking time by 60%
  • Financial habits improved due to automation
