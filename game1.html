The Breathing Void

A calm ritual where your breath fills the empty space.

4–2–6 Breathing
One Player
No Score · Just Calm

Sit comfortably. Look at the glowing circle inside the void. Your only task is to let your breath follow its rhythm: inhale as it grows, hold when it stills, exhale as it shrinks.

There is nothing to reach, nothing to prove. Just you, your lungs, and a piece of quiet space that belongs only to you.

Tip: Use headphones, dim the lights, and stay for at least 1 minute. Your nervous system will thank you.
